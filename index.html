ABOUT


Hi, my name is Navnit and I’m a UX Designer based in San Francisco, CA. Although I wasn’t always a designer, I’ve always had a sense of creativity, empathy, and desire to help others. Growing up, I enjoyed activities like dancing, volunteering, or art which helped me connect with people in different ways. Taking this skillset, I studied business administraion and did various marketing roles for startups and mid size companies, where I learned about the branding process. My experience lead me to follow a career path in UI/UX design. I dedicated myself to learning the design process from surveys to protyping, how to connect with users, and how to create innovative and visually appealing solutions, based on research. To learn more, you can contact me below and I look forward to hearing from you.
