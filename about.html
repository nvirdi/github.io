Hi, I’m Navnit -

A UX Designer based in San Francisco with a passion for delightful designs that impact people





DESIGN THINKING

Although design wasn’t initially on my journey, I naturally found myself in design oriented roles without even knowing it.For example, as a media coordinator for a creative agency, I helped connect car brands with online auto influencers. However, our program was not growing and content not engaging. Seeing this problem, I thought, why not reach out to non auto influencers and create new content. I started reaching out to popular beauty bloggers (I know, how do you connect makeup with cars?), who surprisingly were open to working with auto brands and presented viral content. From there, I worked with the creative team on redesigning our outreach process. I found it exciting to work on such a challenge, yet I wasn’t sure if a career in design was for me.




WHY DESIGN MATTERS

Fast forward to my most recent role at Sutter Health, I found myself again, drawn to solving problems using design. As a project coordinator, I performed data analysis for hospital programs. At the same time, directors were reaching out to me for help with design projects: new office layouts, patient surveys, program pamphlets, and more. I eventually started working with a team of engineers and physicians to build a Sutter mobile app for patients to navigate through the hospital easier and faster. My role went from Excel tasks to helping solve a real patient experience problem, using design. Feeling inspired, I started to rethink my career goals and decided to learn more about UX design.




THE DESIGN LEAP

I submerged myself into a design apprenticeship program where I created responsive and engaging applications, while mentored by experienced, senior designers. From wireframes to creating intuitive prototypes based on research, I continuously improve my skills to be a well rounded designer. So as I mentioned before, design wasn’t initially on my journey, but I've always been designing. I aim to work for an organization that lets me design solutions for user problems in a collaborative and growing environment.
