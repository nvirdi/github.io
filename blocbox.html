Blocbox


Blocbox is a SaaS web application allowing people to collect and store information in one space. Users can create simple documents, store inspiring images or quotes, and add links to websites while collaborating with others.




PROBLEM

When it comes to saving images there are many cloud based platforms to use, however they can have confusing dashboards or lack certain sharing capabilities.

SOLUTION

Blocbox lets users collect images, notes and links while colloborating with others. Users can experience a more simple and intuitive way to organize information.



Research

After reviewing what Blocbox is and the design requirements, a survey to understand the ideal user was distributed to over 50 respondants. The survey focused on how users currently save and share content and their likes and dislikes. Below are the main findings:

90% Browsed online for social media, followed by news
90% Take notes for personal and business items
70% Save content by downloading or bookmarking
80% Work in a collaborative environment; tech or design
70% Found current applications easy to use but limited sharing
60% Use social media to sign up for accounts

Survey results showed most users came from a tech or design background who want to organize their saved content and share with different people. The survey results also indicated the importance of social sharing which was implemented in the final dashboard and homepage designs. Testing

Prior to applying the color palette, wireframes were created to visualize the user flow. Below are two examples of wireframes that were created, the landing page and account settings page. Within the landing page, it was important to highlight the features, pricing and calls to action so the user can get started right away. Within the account settings page, it was also important to highlight the different account types so the user can easily upgrade.



These wireframes were further developed based on user feedback. Users found the landing page slightly confusing and the account settings page too busy with input fields. Taking this feedback, the landing page was modified to list features in a row for better readability. The account settings page was also modified to include a modal box, showing less input fields on the page and reducing confusion. The full wireframe prototype can be viewed below:

Users were asked to navigate through the wireframes and let me know what they expect to happen. Users were given the task to sign up for Blocbox, Add a piece of content and upgrade their account from Free to Professional. Below is the feedback received which was applied to the final design and prototype.


Add Image, Note or Link to Dashboard:

  • Users preferred a search bar to find images
  • Screen doesn't always fit - ensure responsiveness
  • Add button is located on right yet users were more inclined to click left

Sign Up/ Upgrade Account:

  • Users preferred reveiws in a row vs changing reviews
  • Users enjoyed the color indicator so they know which account they have
  • Users enjoyed sign up options on landing page and account settings only
