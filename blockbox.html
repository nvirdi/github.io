BLOCBOX

Research | Branding | Wireframes | Prototype


Blocbox is a SaaS web application that lets you collect and store information in one space. You can create simple documents, store inspiring images or quotes, and add links to websites while collaborating with others.


VIEW PROTOTYPE







PROBLEM

Everyone uses information differently, whether it's saving an inspring image or sharing a link, organizing information can be difficult. There are many cloud based solutions to use, however they can have confusing dashboards or lack certain sharing capabilities.

SOLUTION

Blocbox lets you collect images, notes and links, while colloborating with others. Keep all of your inspiring content in one place and share with specific people or groups. You can experience a more simple and intuitive way to organize information without feeling overwhelmed.













RESEARCH

After reviewing what Blocbox is and the design requirements, a survey to understand the ideal user was distributed to over 50 respondants. The survey focused on how users currently save and share content and their likes and dislikes. Below are the main findings:



90% Browse information for work and news
90% Take notes for personal and business items
70% Download or bookmark information
80% Work in a team based environment
70% Found current apps limited
60% Use social media to sign up for accounts

COMPETITIVE ANALYSIS

After getting to know the users, it was clear they want to save, share, and organize their items in one place. There are many platforms out there similar to Blocbox, however each are designed for different users. You can view the full SWOT analysis of each product below:


Box is a platform for teams to collaborate as one, targeted to business teams or entreprenuers.

Strengths

  • Clear call to action

Weaknesses

  • Overwhelming amount of content

Egnyte is a platform for file sharing and collaboration management, targeted to business teams.

Strengths

  • Success stories
  • Smart reporting and auditing tools

Weaknesses

  • Ineffective call to action
  • Does not include certain markets

CloudApp is a screen recording tool allowing users to edit screenshots and share them.

Strengths

  • Easy to use with Slack integration

Weaknesses

WIREFRAMES

Prior to applying the color palette, wireframes were created to visualize the user flow. The landing page below is an example of my wireframe process. Within the landing page, it was important to highlight the features, pricing and calls to action so the user can get started right away. Users found the landing page slightly confusing and the account settings page too busy with input fields. Taking this feedback, the landing page was modified to list features in a row for better readability.







Another example is within the account settings page where it was important to highlight the different account types so the user can easily upgrade. Users found this page slightly confusing and didn't quickly notice their account type. Taking this feedback, the account settings page was modified to include a modal box, showing less input fields on the page and reducing confusion. All wireframes can be viewed below:

TESTING

Users were asked to navigate through the wireframes and let me know what they expect to happen. Users were given the task to sign up for Blocbox, add a piece of content and upgrade their account from Free to Professional. Below is the user feedback which was applied to the final design and prototype.



Add Image, Note or Link to Dashboard

  • Unresponsive sections
  • Users preferred a search bar to find images
  • Add button located right,users suggested left

Sign Up and Upgrade Account

  • Users enjoyed easy sign up opportunities
  • Users wanted a color indicator for account status
  • Users preferred reveiws in a row vs changing reviews




Using this feedback, I learned that not everything I expect will happen and how essential it is to ideate quickly based on new information. When building the final prototype, I redesigned the account settings page to indicate the account types better as well as change the Add button location and add a search bar.
